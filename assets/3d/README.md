`earth-blue-marble.jpg` is derived from NASA Earth Observatory's July 2004
Blue Marble: Next Generation global base map.

- Source: https://science.nasa.gov/earth/earth-observatory/blue-marble-next-generation/base-map/

- Usage guidance: https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-brand-center/images-and-media/

The mobile asset was resized to 2048 × 1024 and given a posterized cartoon
palette, flat blue oceans, and coastline outlines. The geographic projection
and coastline shapes remain based on the NASA source.
