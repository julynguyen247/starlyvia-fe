# Earth texture credit

`earth-blue-marble.jpg` is derived from NASA Earth Observatory's July 2004
Blue Marble: Next Generation global base map.

- Source: https://science.nasa.gov/earth/earth-observatory/blue-marble-next-generation/base-map/
- Credit: NASA Earth Observatory
- Usage guidance: https://www.nasa.gov/nasa-brand-center/images-and-media/

The mobile asset was resized to 2048 × 1024 and converted into a flat two-color
cartoon map: blue water and green land. It contains no satellite surface detail,
terrain shading, or artificial coastline stroke. The geographic projection and
coastline shapes remain based on the NASA source.
